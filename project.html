Supernova Technology

Internship Project

In the summer of 2019, I interned for a fintech startup in Chicago called Supernova Technology. Supernova Technology specializes in securities based lending solutions and provides these solutions through their digital lending software. At the time I was there, the company was nearing the end stages of redesigning their website. With the launch of their new website, they also wanted to redesign their brochures, one-pagers, and any other sort of media that would be shown to potential customers.

The media I was tasked with redesigning were the company one-pagers. The one-pagers were informational brochures which detailed the features of each of the key components to Supernova's securities based lending digital software. In total, there were 6 one-pagers, one for each key component to their digital lending software. The previous one-pagers, pictures aboved, were lacking visually and did not match their new website's aesthetic. Thus, drawing from the visual cues used to design the new website, I redesigned the one-pagers to match the aesthetic. My work was done in Sketch, a vector graphics editing software.
